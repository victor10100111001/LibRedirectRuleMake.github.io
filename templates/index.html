LibRedirect Rule Maker

What can this tool do?

This tool allows you to easily make uBlock Origin rules for all of the instances in LibRedirect. You can choose what type of protocol and what type of content you would like to block on those websites

Why should I do this?

Disabling JavaScript, Cosmetic Filtering and Remote Fonts can greatly improve your privacy and security. JavaScript is for example often used to fingerprint your webbrowser or perform 0-day exloits. You can see your own browser fingerprint on amiunique.org or coveryourtracks.eff.org.

How do I import the rules in uBlock Origin

First press the uBlock Origin extension icon and then the three gears in the bottom right corner. Inside the dashboard choose "My rules" and then click "Import from file". Choose the file that you have downloaded and press "commit" to make the rules permanent.
